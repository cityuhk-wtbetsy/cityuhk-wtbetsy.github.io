Staying Safe from Cyber-Attacks
Beware of Email Impersonation
  • Emails originating from non-CityU accounts but disguised as sent by CityU senior management
  • Numerous cases reported to Central IT
  • Recipients commonly asked for personal information or purchase of untraceable gift cards
  • Protect yourself against suspicious / phishing emails:
    • Verify the real sender email address (likely not displayed on default screen on mobile phones)
    • Check if the email content is reasonable, e.g. contact by email only
    • Invoke Report Phishing function provided in the email platform
    • Delete it without replying or forwarding
