Security
How to Report Suspicious Emails to Central IT
  • Select the suspicious email on the email list of your Outlook Inbox
  • Click on the Report Spam button at the far right under the "Home" menu
  • Click on the REPORT SPAM button in the popup window to confirm
  • Delete the suspicious email
