How to Report Suspicious Emails to Central IT

Outlook

  • Select the suspicious email on the email list of your Outlook Inbox
  • Click on the Report Spam button at the far right under the "Home" menu
  • Click on the REPORT SPAM button in the popup window to confirm
  • Delete the suspicious email

Google Mail

  • Attach the email and send to reportspam@cityu.edu.hk
  • Delete the suspicious email
Staff, Students and Alumni
Watch Out for Phishing and Scamming Emails
  • ALWAYS check if sender's address is legitimate. Contact sender to verify using another channel (e.g. phone call) if needed
  • NEVER click on any website links or open any attachments unless you are certain it is safe
  • NEVER provide any login credentials, personal or financial information
  • ALWAYS report suspicious emails to us and then delete them. NEVER forward them to other colleagues
  • Example of a suspicious email
  • Learn more about email security (visit Training and awareness page)
